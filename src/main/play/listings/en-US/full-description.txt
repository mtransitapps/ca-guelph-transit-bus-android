This application adds Guelph Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the planned schedule as well as real time service statuses and news from guelph.ca and @guelphtransit on Twitter.

Guelph Transit buses serves Guelph in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section below or by following this Google Play link https://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-guelph-transit-bus-android

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by City Of Guelph and Guelph Transit.
http://open.guelph.ca/dataset/guelph-transit-gtfs-data/

This app is not related with City Of Guelph and Guelph Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from guelph.ca and Twitter

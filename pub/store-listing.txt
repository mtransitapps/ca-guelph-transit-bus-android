Full description:
This application adds Guelph Transit buses information to MonTransit BETA.

This app contains the buses schedule (available offline) and news from guelph.ca and @guelphtransit on Twitter.

BETA: Thanks for your patience while the last features are being added and the last bugs are being fixed.

Guelph Transit serves Guelph in Ontario, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit BETA app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit BETA app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by City Of Guelph and Guelph Transit.
http://open.guelph.ca/dataset/guelph-transit-gtfs-data/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-guelph-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with City Of Guelph and Guelph Transit.

Permissions:
- Other: required to read news from guelph.ca and Twitter
